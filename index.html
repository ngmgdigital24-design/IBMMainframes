FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before joining us.

Who can join this course? Do I need prior IT experience?

This course is designed for freshers (B.E/B.Tech/B.Sc/BCA graduates), MCA students, and working IT professionals who want to upskill. No prior Mainframe experience is required — we start from absolute zero and take you to an industry-ready level.

Is the placement assistance really 100%? How does it work?

Yes! Our placement cell works with 150+ partner companies and conducts regular placement drives. We provide referrals, interview scheduling, and negotiation support. We continue supporting you until you secure a job — not just for the 6-month duration of the course.

Is training online, offline, or both?

We offer both online (live instructor-led via Zoom/Teams) and offline classroom training at our Hyderabad center. All sessions are recorded and available for lifetime access so you can revise anytime.

Will I get access to a real IBM Mainframe environment?

Absolutely. All students get access to our dedicated IBM Mainframe environment (z/OS emulator with Hercules, plus cloud-based IBM Z environment). You'll run real JCL jobs, write COBOL programs, and execute DB2 queries on actual systems.

What are the EMI options available?

We offer 0% interest EMI for 6–12 months through partner NBFCs and banks. For the combo course at ₹55,000, you can pay as low as ₹5,500/month. We also offer an early-bird discount and scholarship for merit students — ask our counselor for details.

What salary can I expect after completing this course?

Freshers typically get placed between ₹4–7 LPA, while working professionals with prior IT experience often see packages of ₹8–15 LPA. Our highest placement so far has been ₹22 LPA for a senior Mainframe + AI specialist role at a US-based MNC's India center.
